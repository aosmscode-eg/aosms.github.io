(ATD) AOSMS To Do Project

Description

AOSMS To Do is a to do app that powered by Encrypted Notepad witch it can encrypt to do list and decrypt to do list with 7 LAYERS OF ENCRYPTION. Written in Python. To download ATD you should press Download ATD

Types of encryption

  1. Caesar Cipher
  2. Base16
  3. Base32
  4. Base64
  5. Standard Base64
  6. Base85
  7. Text Reserving
